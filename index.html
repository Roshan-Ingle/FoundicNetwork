About Company

Foundic is a founder-focused platform that helps early-stage startups solve major business problems like sales, growth, go-to-market, fundraising, and scaling through structured consultation.

All guidance is provided by professionals with a minimum of 8+ years of real industry experience, ensuring practical, execution-level insight, not just theory.

Currently, Foundic operates in a consultation-first model during the pre-registration phase. After legal registration, the platform will also support execution, where solutions are implemented under a structured system with monitoring and accountability.

The Gap & Our Bridge

01

The Problem

Many founders are part of multiple startup communities and platforms, but still do not get deep, structured consultation for their exact business problems like sales, growth, funding, hiring, and scaling at the right time.

Focused Consultation

Foundic provides structured, one-to-one and small-group consultation to founders, where their exact business problem is deeply understood and solved with clear strategy and direction, instead of generic advice from large communities.

Guided Execution
